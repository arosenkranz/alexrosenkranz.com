This year I've brought back my monthly Kranzjams playlist. There's no rhyme or reason to the order or theme of the songs, though you could probably infer some things based on pockets of songs added throughout the month.

Standout track, for me, is [Midnight by Solaris Heights](https://open.spotify.com/track/5XNpzvtxVZJ7kSKb8opMgz). It's such a groove, not sure how I never knew about this song. I found it recently as I've been going through all of the old fabric record label mixes and found this track on [Hipp-E and Halo - fabric 07](https://store.fabriclondon.com/products/fabric-07).

While you wait for the next Kranzjams playlist, checkout my [Spotify profile](https://open.spotify.com/user/alexrosenkranz?si=06560139e3f346aa) for a ton more music to discover.
